About This Project

This recipe generator was a kind of selfish endeavor, but maybe others would find it useful too!

Each recipe is curated from a personal collection and organized by meal type — breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks.

It randomly pulls a recipe from a Google Sheet every time you click, helping reduce choice paralysis and make meal planning a little more fun!

Made with Flask + Bootstrap
