📚 About This Project

👋 Hey there! I'm Katie (@ohitsmekatie), and this little site is a way to get recipes without endless scrolling. It's also a way for my friends and I to create a shared database of recipes we love.

📋 Every recipe is pulled from a curated list in a shared Google Sheet. You can add to the sheet directly or submit a recipe with a link from the "Add a recipe" section of the site.

🎲 Use the generator to get a random recipe by meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks).
